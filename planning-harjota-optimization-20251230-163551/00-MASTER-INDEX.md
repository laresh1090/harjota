## Discovery & Analysis

### Discovery Questioning Session
Through structured discovery questions, we established:
- **Implementation Timeframe**: 6-month balanced approach (not 3-month sprint or 12-month deep transformation)
- **Target Market**: Multi-sector approach (SMEs, NGOs, Government) rather than single vertical
- **Entry Product**: IDIA (Institutional Digital Intelligence Audit) as primary conversion point
- **Proof Strategy**: 2-3 discounted pilot projects for immediate case study development
- **Website Urgency**: Site must be ready TODAY to support immediate business needs

### Exploration Analysis (5 Subagent Reports)

**Agent 1: Homepage Messaging & Positioning**
- Identified ICP clarity gap (visitors unclear who Harjota serves)
- Found 70% abstract/conceptual messaging vs 30% outcome-focused
- Recommended Problem/Value Statement section and ICP context additions
- **Key Finding**: Operating principles need translation to tangible outcomes

**Agent 2: Services Page Structure**
- Assessed current 3-tier audit package structure (₦300K-₦5M)
- Identified missing IDIA branding and retainer advisory model
- Found disconnect between service tiers and engagement pathway
- **Key Finding**: Services are partially productized but lack clear pathway progression

**Agent 3: Products Page Effectiveness**
- Analyzed Academic Intelligence System (AIS) presentation
- Found generic value proposition and buried differentiation
- Identified weak CTA strategy and "In Development" perception issues
- **Key Finding**: AIS needs stronger positioning as flagship platform product

**Agent 4: UI/UX & Design Patterns**
- Assessed visual hierarchy as Medium (5/10) and interactivity as Low (3/10)
- Found missing framework diagrams and process visuals (strategic requirement)
- Identified text-heavy design with minimal visual relief
- **Key Finding**: Static presentation lacks engagement modern B2B expects

**Agent 5: Credibility & Trust Signals**
- Found strong methodology showcase and framework transparency
- Identified critical gaps: no case studies, testimonials, or client logos
- Discovered disconnected team social links and minimal founder bios
- **Key Finding**: Can build credibility TODAY through founder credentials and process transparency

## Risk Management

### Critical Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Pilot Projects Don't Sell**
- *Mitigation*: Outreach to 30+ prospects, offer 50-70% discount, target warm network first
- *Contingency*: Internal case studies, pro bono for strategic partners

**Risk 2: Content Production Falls Behind**
- *Mitigation*: Batch content creation, repurpose across channels, use templates
- *Contingency*: Hire freelance writer ($500-1,000/month)

**Risk 3: Founder Bandwidth Overwhelmed**
- *Mitigation*: Prioritize ruthlessly, automate admin tasks, hire VA early
- *Contingency*: Extend timelines by 2-4 weeks, reduce content frequency

**Risk 4: SEO Results Slow**
- *Mitigation*: Focus on long-tail keywords, local SEO quick wins, quality backlinks
- *Contingency*: Supplement with paid ads (Google/LinkedIn), partnership marketing

**Risk 5: Competitive Pressure Increases**
- *Mitigation*: Differentiate through IP, thought leadership, superior delivery
- *Contingency*: Niche down to specific sector (education or healthcare first)
